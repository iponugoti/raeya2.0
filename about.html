My name is Raeya Ponugoti. I recently graduated from SCAD with a B.F.A. in Film and Television and a minor in photography.

My vision is my intuition. I have a strong sense of visual style that I contribute to every project I work on.

I dream of revolutionizing the film industry in order to provide equal opportunities and recognition for underrepresented creators. My goal is to create a space for discourse about the role of women in film and the importance of female-told stories.

You can reach me at ponugotiraeya@gmail.com.
